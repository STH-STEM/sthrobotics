About St Helena Robotics

Look at St Helena Robotics in more detail

Introduction

We provide creative and innovative students at St Helena School extraordinary and exciting experiences and opportunities through the means of constructing robots and competing in numerous tournaments. Currently, we primarily offer both VEX IQ & VEX EDR robotics platforms to most students as well as Lego Mindstorms as an alternative to others. Participants win not only on the game field, but also off the field, where they'll find themselves developing core teamwork and communication skills that will prove to be especially beneficial in later life. STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering & Maths) didn't always have to be boring!

VEX Robotics

The VEX IQ and VEX EDR platforms and competition programmes, provided by VEX Robotics in parntership with the RECF (Robotics Education and Competition Foundation) provide individuals with the tools they need to have in order to build robots according to the current season's game and compete with them, developing teamwork and communication skills along the way.
