Introduction

St Helena Robotics hosts numerous VEX Robotics Competition and VEX IQ Challenge teams each season - the specifics for 2020-2021 will be announced soon! You'll be able to visit some of our teams' pages for more information such as team members and statistics like scores and events attended. Teams meet every Monday to program and drive their robots, ensuring they're up to scratch for their next event.

There will be a delay in the announcing of teams for the 2020-2021 season due to school closures. Please check back soon.
