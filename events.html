Introduction

At St Helena Robotics we aim to host attend at least two events for each competition programme (VIQC/VRC), as well as hosting one or two large-scale qualifying events of our own. On event days, students will collaborate with other schools and prove their skills in numerous qualifying and finals matches. Specifics for each game can be found here.
