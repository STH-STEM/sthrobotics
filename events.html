Introduction

At St Helena Robotics we aim to host attend at least two events for each competition programme (VIQC/VRC), as well as hosting one or two large-scale UK Nationals qualifying events of our own. On event days, students will collaborate with other schools and prove their skills in numerous qualifying and finals matches, though specifics will depend on what programme the event is for, but more can be found out about that here.
