VEX EDR

VEX EDR is considered to be the premier educational robotics solution, in which all of our EDR members would agree. Students utilise real-life concepts and ideas and adapt them to create a fully functioning robot whilst developing teamwork and communication skills, often without them even knowing.

The EDR platform is metal-based and requires skills with multiple tools to construct a sturdy contraption. The coding aspect of VEX EDR presents our students with evolving challenges which they work to overcome as they grow more confident with proper coding languages. The VEX EDR platform develops vital skills that will prove beneficial for every student participating in the programme.

This season the VEX Robotics Competition is "Tower Takeover". You can learn about VRC "Tower Takeover" and other EDR related products below.

VEX IQ

VEX IQ is our most popular competition platform at St Helena Robotics. It consists of creative, innovative and enthusiastic Year 7s and 8s working to build multiple robots in this season's game, VEX IQ Challenge "Squared Away".

Contraptions can be constructed in just minutes thanks to the snap-together parts, it allows students to learn about different aspects of science without them even realising it. Furthermore, there are multiple coding options for beginners and those looking for a challenge, which makes the system completely approachable, even for those who've never came across coding previously.
