Introduction

At St Helena Robotics we host six teams, four VEX Robotics Challenge teams and two taking part in the VEX IQ Challenge. You'll be able to visit some of our teams' pages for more information*, such as team members and statistics like scores and events attended. Teams meet every Monday to program and drive their robots, ensuring they're up to scratch for their next event. The following information is relevant to the 2019-2020 season as September.
