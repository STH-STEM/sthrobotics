At a Glance

At St Helena Robotics, we provide innovative and creative students with new, exciting and amazing experiences to develop their enthusiasm and skills within the academic STEM (Science, Technlology, Engineering and Maths) fields. Through these robotics programmes, students build on beneficial life skills such as teamwork and communication, all whilst having fun through building and competing with custom robots they have built themselves.
