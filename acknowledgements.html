Acknowledgements

St Helena Robotics wouldn't be where it is today without the efforts and dedication of
many people, inside and outside of the club

Mr W. James

Senior Science Technician

Mr James is the man behind the club. He sorts everything from ensuring that students know how to get started to bringing the teams and their robots to the number of events St Helena Robotics competes at each year. He has a contagious passion and enthusiam for STEM, also hosting and helping out with St Helena School's other STEM offerings: Arduino Club, STEM Club, and Drone Club.

He's dedicated hundreds of hours of his time (if not thousands) to volunteering and getting more people intrested in STEM. Mr James has enabled students to maximise the performance of their robots, and continues to selflessly and thoroughly mentor each team to help each individual realise their full potential.

On behalf of the entire club, we can't thank you enough for everything you've done for us and everything you continue to do.

Chris Calver

Head of Education, VEX Robotics UK

Chris has closely cooperated with St Helena Robotics and with the school for many, many years now. He, like us, loves STEM and he has been mentoring teams, hosting events, and publishing online programming tutorials, amongst the many other ways he's been helping the STEM community.

Lately, he's been especially helpful with us beginning to run our own events from beginning to end, the first of which was the 2019 Essex Christmas Clash.

This doesn't even cover his contributions to St Helena School's other STEM offerings, and we thank him so much for all of his dedication and efforts.

Everyone who's supported us

We'd like to thank everyone who's shown us support on our journey. From the people who've liked a post from us on Twitter, to those parents who've contributed to the club, you enable us to keep pushing harder to achieve our goals. Thank you.
